About Us

Welcome to Kehilas Lev V'Nefesh Nestled in the heart of Bergenfield, NJ, Kehilas Lev V'Nefesh stands as a beacon of warmth, acceptance, and spiritual growth. Our Mission

At Kehilas Lev V'Nefesh, we believe that the doors to spirituality and community should be open to all, regardless of background. Our mission is simple yet profound: to create a space where everyone feels welcome, and where the heart and soul of Judaism are not just taught but deeply felt and lived. We are committed to fostering a supportive environment that encourages meaningful connections, spiritual exploration, and personal growth.

Our Beginnings

Founded during the challenging times of COVID-19, Kehilas Lev V'Nefesh emerged as a response to the community's yearning for a more inclusive, heart-centered place of worship. Our founders envisioned a shul where the emphasis on the heart and soul of each individual would shine brightly, creating a spiritual home that felt both comforting and inspiring. This vision has guided us from our inception and continues to illuminate our path forward.

Our Community

Kehilas Lev V'Nefesh is proud to be a diverse community that welcomes individuals and families from all walks of life. We believe in the beauty of diversity and the strength it brings to our collective journey. While we may not highlight specific programs or activities at this time, our doors are always open to ideas, initiatives, and volunteers looking to contribute to the vibrant tapestry of our community.

Join Us

Whether you are seeking a place for prayer, looking for a community to celebrate life's milestones, or simply searching for a spiritual home where you can be yourself, Kehilas Lev V'Nefesh invites you to join us. Together, we will continue to build a shul where the heart and soul of every member are nurtured and celebrated.
